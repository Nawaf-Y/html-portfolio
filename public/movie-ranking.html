The Best Anime/Mangas According to Nawaf

My top Anime/Mangas of all-time.

Hunter X Hunter

This is hands down my favorite Manga and Anime series of all time.

Jujutsu Kaisen

This is my favorite for the more recent manga series out right now and of course my favorite character is Satoro Gojo aka Adult Killua.

Naruto series (Including shippuden)

This is one of my very first animes that I genuinly loved, The story of Naruto Uzamaki truly resonated with me from such a young age and I honestly would recommend starting here if I wanted anyone to get into Anime.

A Silent Voice

This is one of the only anime movies that I believe should have one an award for best movie.

The Promised Neverland

Very mind-bending plot that will have you on the edge of your seat every single episode wondering whats going to happen next.

Classroom of the Elite

A psychological thriller that really made me question the capabilities of the human mind
